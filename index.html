The story of hidden letter

Maria was fascinated by fat, black cat.

It was sitting high on the tree and making so terryfing sound that nobody could pass him without the glance. She noticed three things though:

  1. People looked disgusted by him.
  2. They wanted to get away as fast as possible.
  3. Nobody thought about helping him out.

Only she did. The problem was that nobody cared about her neither.

Her spirits drooped for good when she read in Daily News the most horrible news that could came up today.
